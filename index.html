Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my water intake?

Tap the drink button on the home screen and select the beverage type. Each drink type counts at a different hydration rate — water counts 100%, while coffee and soda count slightly less. Your animal fills up as you get closer to your daily goal.

How do I unlock new animals?

Hatch eggs to discover new animals! Standard eggs are earned through daily hydration streaks and reaching your goals. Premium eggs, which unlock rarer animals, are available with a Water Animals Pro subscription.

How do I change my daily hydration goal?

Go to Settings (the gear icon) and adjust your daily goal in milliliters or ounces. You can also switch between metric and imperial units from the same screen.

What is Water Animals Pro?

Water Animals Pro is a subscription that unlocks all premium animals and exclusive egg types. It is available as a monthly or yearly plan and can be managed at any time through your Apple ID subscription settings.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Water Animals. From there you can cancel, change, or review your plan. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

Inside the app, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to originally purchase the subscription.

Why isn't my streak continuing?

Streaks require you to meet your daily hydration goal each day. Logging some water but not reaching your goal will not extend the streak. Make sure you log all your drinks before midnight.

How does the weather adjustment work?

Water Animals uses WeatherKit to detect your local temperature. On hot days, your recommended intake may increase slightly to help you stay properly hydrated.

How do I set up reminders?

Go to Settings and enable Reminders. You can customize your wake time, sleep time, and how often you want to be nudged throughout the day.
